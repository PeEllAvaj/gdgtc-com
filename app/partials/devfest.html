Google DevFest Twin Cities!

Feb 8th, 2014.
UST Shultze School of Entrepreneurship, downtown MPLS.

About

What

Google is promoting the idea worldwide of regional chapters hosting DevFest Events. Many other cities have had good success with them.

When

All day, Feb 8th, 2014

Where

Shultze School of Entrepreneurship, Downtown MPLS. Easy parking, great space. Plenty of breakout rooms.

Format

The plan is to kick off the day with an awesome Keynote Speaker, after some coffee and treats. Then to breakout into simultaneous sessions of varying format. Either presentation style, panel style, or a workshop. Presentations will be in depth on variety of topics by experts in the subject matter. The workshops will be hands on events where participants will be able to experience working with the topic and skills presented.

Speaker FAQ

We're planning on at least two tracks, Android and Web Development, but have the capacity and interest to add others (dataviz, analytics, cloud services, arduino, etc). If you have a great tech idea that you'd like to talk about, or run a workshop of, we'd love to hear about it. Topics are not required to be specifically about a Google technology.
